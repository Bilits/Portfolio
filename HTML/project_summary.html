Crypto Simulator Platform

Posted by Moe on March 1 2021

CryptoSim is a cryptocurrency simulator that provides a platform for trading various cryptocurrencies using paper trade and to simulate and predict all the possible risk and profit.


Crypto Simulator Platform

Cryptocurrency market prediction is the act of trying to determine the future value of a coin or other financial instrument traded on a financial exchange. Cryptocurrency price prediction can provide a lending hand to cryptocurrency investors for making proper investment decisions in order to acquire higher profits. The successful prediction of a cryptocurrency’s future price could yield significant profit. The cryptocurrency market is not an efficient market. all investors do not get all information at the same time and the time it takes to evaluate information before they act differs between investors. Many investors do not show rational behavior. Greed and fear are strong feelings and may result in panic sales and crypto market bubbles. Therefore, in order to regulate the crypto market to obtain maximum profit or achieve a certain goal in general without being victimized by inconsistencies, predicting crypto behavior is a top priority.


This project analyzes and compares the ability of different algorithms using blockchain technology to predict price fluctuations for three cryptocurrencies: bitcoin, litecoin and ethereum. Users can create accounts and by investing unreal money based on these algorithm-based robots, they can see the result of trade and find the algorithm which had the best result.

  • Accurate predictions to assist cryptocurrency investors towards right investing
  • Simulated trade platform using paper trading that allows an investor to practice buying and selling without risking real money
  • Compare the most reliable indicator on a chosen cryptocurrency to define the risk ratio
  • lead to potential increased profits and reduce risks


Project Vision:


“Our vision is to provide a platform which will lead our investors to potential increased profit” Cryptocurrency price prediction can provide a lending hand to cryptocurrency investors for making proper investment decisions in order to acquire higher profits while it can also support policy decision-making and financial researchers for studying cryptocurrency markets behavior.
