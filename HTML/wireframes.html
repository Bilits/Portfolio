Wireframes/Mock ups

Landing page:


Landing page is where the user will first be redirected to, from here user will have access to either sign in or sign up to the platform. There is also some useful information in this page about the platform and what we do

Join us:


After user click on sign up button they will be redirected to this page where they can choose to be a part of professional members or beginners. Professional members will have access to all the features on the website such as: Prediction, Trading Bot, Paper trading

While the beginners will have access to only paper trading and viewing the live market. They can practice buy and selling cryptocurrencies virtually with real-time price

Registeration:


After they choose which types of account they are signing up for, they will be redirected to this page where it asks for their personal information such as: Email, Phone number, Password and so on

Login:


User will receive an email including a verification code. after they registered the account successfully through that link, they will be redirected to this page to login to their personal dashboard

Account:


Dashboard contains several pages which some are accessible for beginners and some are not. This page is the account of the user. User will get information about their virtual money, their transaction and anything related to their orders

Prediction:


Prediction page is only accessible for professional user. They will receive notification when it's a good time to buy in to the market or its a good time to sell out their cryptocurrency. NOTE that they can use our paper trading features to test the application and then move their transaction to a real exchange platform
